Choose Your Own Adventure Project!

Wednesday, February 7, 2024

The goal of this project was to make our own adventure on the topic that we were assigned. In this project we are expected to make a 10+ file adventure using internal links that linked all the markdown files together. This was a fun project that allow us to practice collaboration with our partners and practice more coding or markdown.

My partner for this project is Nayer E. For the Choose Your Own Adventure project (CYOA) we were assigned "The Game Of Life". For this project we were expected to have a CYOA plan,internal links to files within the repo, minimum of 10 markdwon files, and collaboration with our partner. Our Game of Life project is about Life(obviously), where people can choose the path they take in life. It starts with the alarming ringing to signify the start of life and they will be able to choose their own path to life. When someone chooses the wrong path or option they will be Bankrupt and life will restart. But if they choose the right path and work hard they will be able to become a millionaire and retire at a young age. Our project focuses on all the right and wrong choices that can put someone high up to the sky or drop them down to the core of the world. There were multiple internal links that brought them to a different file everytime they click on a choice. This was a great opportunity to work on collaborating with others.
